Welcome to FedTrain (Former Site)

For over three decades FedTrain provided tailored financial management and appropriations law training to scores of federal agencies and tens of thousands of federal executives, managers and administrators.

In December 2022 FedTrain ceased to do business and closed its doors. It has been a amazing experience for us and we are forever grateful to the organizations that chose us to conduct this important training for them.

If you have contacted us in search of a replacement training certificate, no worries.  We have copies of certificates issued after January 1, 1999.

  • Email to registrar@fedtrain.net

  • Your name, the email address you were using, the approximate month and year of your class and the name of your federal agency hosting the training

  • Allow 10-14 working days for us to get it to you.

Best wishes to you in all your future endeavors.
